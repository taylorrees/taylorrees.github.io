Introduction

Established by Darren Daniel in early 2012, DD Plumbers prides itself on resolving your problems with passion, integrity and honesty. From day one, our goal has been to bring professional plumbing services into the hands of every individual. Located in Llanelli, South Wales we provide plumbing, heating and gas solutions to Carmarthenshire and the surrounding areas.

Learn More

Who are we

We are a growing, passionate and powerful team who have fine tuned our skills in order to master our craft. We take plumbing seriously. As a result, DD Plumbers has developed an established client list who trust us to provide work of the highest standard. We pride ourselves on developing an unmatched working relationship with our clients so that we can be there for you when you need us.

With returning customers accounting for over half of our annual work, customer satisfaction is of the highest priority. We feel that we deliver some of the best in the business, but we'd like you to decide that for yourself.

Our services

Whilst some people try to be good at many things, here at DD Plumbers we aim to be great at one thing. Plumbing. Our local plumbers are gas safe registered, fully licensed and insured. We've helped hundreds of customers across South West Wales with their plumbing needs.

We are extremely focused on being the best solution to your plumbing, heating or gas related requirements. Our team will be there for you along every step of your journey and with our 24 hour emergency call-out service you can rest assured that when you need a plumber, we are already on our way.

How we work

At DD Plumbers we implement a structured, transparent pricing system that ensures the cost of your job will always be individually assessed. We use a specific set of criteria to ensure that every job we price is as fair as the next, removing the guesswork and reassuring you that every aspect of your job has been considered.

We feel that clarity is essential to any successful process. In light of this, we will always provide our customers with a thorough breakdown of the work to be carried out, thus keeping you and our team on the same page. It is because of this methodic working style that DD Plumbers has established an enviable reputation that few companies are able to match.

Where are we

We are based in Llanelli, but we love to travel. Members of our team can often be found fixing leaks and installing boilers in and around Llanelli, Swansea, Cardiff, Carmarthen, Pembroke and the majority of the South West Wales area. With our fleet of vans on the road daily you're never out of reach.
