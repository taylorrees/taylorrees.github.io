DISCOVER CSA

Introduction

Established in Llanelli, South Wales in 1992, the CSA Service Group quickly developed a loyal base of clients. Over the years a lot has changed, but here at CSA we pride ourselves on maintaining the same level of client relations that we have had from day one. We constantly strive to provide our customers with the best services that are available on the market today.

Services

Recruitment

CSA Recruitment offers clients a time saving and cost effective solution to temporary, seasonal or permanent workforce requirements adding value to any business.

Securicall

Securicall Guarding provides industry leading SIA ACS manned security and guard services throughout the UK. Our team of highly trained personnel are licensed under strict SIA regulations providing our clients highest quality security services.

Hygiene

CSA Hygiene is a market leading supplier of Industrial and Commercial Cleaning. Within CSA Hygiene we have the knowledge, experience and manpower to solve any Hygiene issue for contractors and businesses of all sizes.

Clients

At CSA we pride ourselves on providing our clients with first class service. No matter how large or small your organisation we always strive to provide you with the highest quality service possible, treating each client as an individual. This is a strategy that we have maintained from day one and it seems to have worked for us so far. Over the years the CSA Group has provided services for some of the industries largest names.

Background

Supporting our local and wider community has become a large part of the day to day running of the CSA Service Group. Llanelli has one of the most diverse populations throughout the whole of the United Kingdom and we openly embrace this here at CSA. To show our support for the community we frequently provide sponsorship to a number of local sports and recreational clubs as well as a large variety of local events. We feel this adds value to our community and this is something we are very proud of. A selection of the clubs and events to which we have provided sponsorships can be seen below.
